ABOUT

Founded in 1992, Sabor Latino Dance Ensemble strives to educate the Cornell University and surrounding Ithaca community about Latino culture through music, dance, and visual arts. We are the first Latino dance troupe on Cornell University's campus and represent our Latin American and Caribbean roots through dances such as Merengue, Plena, Mambo, Bachata, and Cumbia, as well as our ties to our experience as Latinos in the U.S. through dances such as Salsa (on 1 and on 2), Latin-jazz, Latin hip-hop, Reggaeton, and Latino house. Through our annual concert, workshops, and community service, we present the significance of music and dance in our cultures.
